A new start

Hi there!
To be quite frank, I have been meaning to start something like this for years.
As I started my humble begininings into the world of computer science, I found the boundless amount of resources on any paradigm or platform to be fruitful and spent years learning from them.
This project intends to paint little pictures of things I have learned / will learn over the years and will mainly just contain rambles on things I find fascinating at the time.
Things I create here SHOULD NOT be seen as a replacement for reading textbooks and other more formal resources if you are learning these concepts for the first time and instead should just be consumed purely for fun.
My website is not pretty nor does it have any pretty animations. I'm sorry; I hate front end. (Maybe eventually I might add some colour tho haha)
I hope this is a project I stick to over the next coming months and doesn't just end up abandoned in the void

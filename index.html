I am currently a Master's student at King's College London, enrolled in the Advanced Computing MSc program.

My research and passion lie in the development and analysis of lattice reduction algorithms, empirical experimentation of lattice-based cryptanalysis methods on classical public-key cryptography such as RSA and post-quantum lattice-based digital signatures. I have a strong foundation in both the complex mathematical underpinnings of lattices and their practical software implementation using libraries such as fplll. My goal is to help organisations transition to these rapidly evolving cryptographic standards, ensuring their systems remain highly efficient while maintaining robust security against quantum-enabled adversaries.
